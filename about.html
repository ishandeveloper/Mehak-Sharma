MEHAK SHARMA

Broadly skilled Doctoral fellow in mechanical and automation engineering with prolific research experience in the field of product design and development, NPD, CAD/CAM, 3D scanning, point cloud computing, rapid prototyping, and programming.

What I Do

Research

I have been working in the field of CAD/CAM since last 11 years. My post graduate research was development of MATLAB based algorithm for surface reconstruction and my doctoral research is based on designing and developing biomechancial prosthesis using cutting age technology such as 3d metal printing.

Design

I am highly skilled in using mechanical CAD softwares CREO (professional certification) and SOLIDWORKS. I also have keen understanding and knowledge of CAE and rapid prototyping tools such as ANSYS, ABAQUS, 3D SLICER, FUSION360 and MeshLab.

Teach

I have a cumulative experience in teaching diploma, undergraduate and post graduate students for 3+ years at college and university level. I have taught subjects (theory and practicals) and managed laboratories in CAD/CAM, Thermodynamics, Physics, Industrial Safety and manufacturing processes.

Write

I am a passionate writer with diversified exposure in technical as well as creative writing. I have successfully written technical reports, scientific papers, projects, articles and thesis in last 10 years as a researcher. I blog about various social and physiological issues through articles, stories and poems. I also review movies and tv series through my blog and earned massive praises on social media for those reviews in the past.
