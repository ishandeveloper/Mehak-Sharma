Hey There!

I am Mehak, a scientist and researcher, currently finishing PhD from IGDTUW, New Delhi. My research project is funded under Women Scientist scheme (WOS-A), Department of Science and Technology, Government of India for a period of three years.

I am passionate about utilizing technology and engineering tools for social well being and upliftment of mankind and thats why my project is based on using softwares like SOLIDWORKS, ANSYS, 3D SLICER and 3D printing to create a sustainable tailor made biomechanical prosthesis. I have successfully designed and developed a custom 3D printed TMJ prosthesis for patients with disabled jaw joints.

I am also extremely ardent and wilful about writing; be it technical or creative. This intense passion stems from deep rooted desire to express myself through writing which i inculcates into everything I write whether it is a report, a technical paper, a thesis, a blog, an article or a poem.
